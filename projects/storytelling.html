01 · Project Overview

The art of storytelling in website design

This project explored how storytelling can transform websites from static collections of information into meaningful digital experiences. Instead of only focusing on layout and visuals, I examined how narrative structure, pacing, emotional engagement, and interaction design can guide users through a website.

The project helped me understand web design as a user-centered and narrative-driven process. Through research and analysis, I studied how storytelling frameworks can improve clarity, engagement, trust, and user perception.

04 · Design Reflection

Design is not only how it looks — it is how it works.

This project changed the way I think about web design. Before completing this artifact, I often approached websites from a visual and technical perspective. After researching storytelling principles, I began to see design as an intentional experience that unfolds over time.

Now, when I design, I ask what story the website is telling, how users are guided through that story, and whether the structure supports clarity, trust, and emotional engagement.

05 · Reflection

Growth as a designer

Completing this project marked an important moment in my growth as a designer. It helped me move beyond thinking of websites as only visual layouts and taught me to consider user experience, narrative flow, interaction, and emotional connection.

The skills developed through this project continue to influence my interest in UX/UI design, front-end development, and Human-Computer Interaction.
