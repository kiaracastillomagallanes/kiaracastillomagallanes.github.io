Cosplay Exhibit Mockup

UI/UX Design

Cosplay Exhibit Mockup

Cosplay Exhibit mockup, I meticulously analyzed usability test data to refine the wireframe of a digital kiosk, aiming to replicate real-world aesthetics and functionality. Centered around a cosplay exhibition theme, the wireframe features a homepage introducing the exhibition and category pages showcasing the exhibition map, history, craftsmanship, and an interactive game. Challenges arose in interpreting user behavior and aligning the design with cultural expectations, prompting a multifaceted approach. Leveraging Adobe XD and Illustrator, I refined visual elements and enhanced user engagement based on feedback and observations. This process illuminated the symbiotic relationship between design, usability, and cultural context, emphasizing the importance of crafting designs that resonate with diverse audiences. Ultimately, the project not only enriched my skill set but also underscored the significance of cultural sensitivity in creating impactful interactive experiences.
