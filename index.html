About me

I'm Kiara Castillo Magallanes

A UI/UX designer and Front-end based in Indy.

I am a Media Arts and Science graduate from IUI, with a specialization in Web Design and Development, and I am currently pursuing a master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction. My work focuses on creating user-centered digital experiences that combine storytelling, UX research, usability, accessibility, and thoughtful visual design.
Over time, I have developed skills in Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, design systems, prototyping, and front-end development. I enjoy transforming ideas into interactive experiences that are not only visually engaging, but also clear, functional, and meaningful for users.
My design approach is guided by empathy, research, and narrative thinking. I believe strong digital experiences should guide users through a story while helping them complete tasks with confidence and ease. I am interested in opportunities where I can continue growing as a UI/UX designer and front-end developer, collaborate with creative teams, and contribute to human-centered solutions.
