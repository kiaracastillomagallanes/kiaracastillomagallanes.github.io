Cosplay Exhibit Usability Test

UI/UX Design

Cosplay Exhibit Usability Test

The Usability Test project focused on evaluating the functionality of a digital kiosk through real user interactions. Leveraging a carefully crafted strategy, participants were recruited from diverse backgrounds to ensure comprehensive feedback reflective of the target demographic. Challenges in recruitment, including geographical constraints and time limitations, were overcome through an extensive outreach strategy utilizing social media, community forums, and local networks. Utilizing Word, a structured usability test was devised to present tasks systematically, maintaining consistency in testing conditions. Observations during the test provided invaluable insights into user behaviors, frustrations, and moments of ease, complemented by feedback gathered through surveys and interviews. The project not only enhanced proficiency in designing effective usability tests but also underscored the importance of nuanced observation techniques in understanding user experiences beyond quantitative data. Ultimately, the project aimed to refine the digital kiosk prototype based on user feedback, contributing to its overall performance and usability.

Cosplay Exhibit Mockup

UI/UX Design

Cosplay Exhibit Mockup

Cosplay Exhibit mockup, I meticulously analyzed usability test data to refine the wireframe of a digital kiosk, aiming to replicate real-world aesthetics and functionality. Centered around a cosplay exhibition theme, the wireframe features a homepage introducing the exhibition and category pages showcasing the exhibition map, history, craftsmanship, and an interactive game. Challenges arose in interpreting user behavior and aligning the design with cultural expectations, prompting a multifaceted approach. Leveraging Adobe XD and Illustrator, I refined visual elements and enhanced user engagement based on feedback and observations. This process illuminated the symbiotic relationship between design, usability, and cultural context, emphasizing the importance of crafting designs that resonate with diverse audiences. Ultimately, the project not only enriched my skill set but also underscored the significance of cultural sensitivity in creating impactful interactive experiences.

Build-A-Bear Rebrand

The Build-A-Bear rebrand project focused on revitalizing the company's identity, employing a range of strategies to achieve this goal.
